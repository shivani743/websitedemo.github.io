Fresh Electrical Supply & Installations


We undertake
  • Design, supply, installation and commissioning, testing, maintenance of regular or customised electrical works.
  • Supply & Installation of machinery and related electrical equipment, instrumentation system and other required accessories under a specific requirement on turnkey basis.
  • Supply & installation of computer networking system, fire alarm system, telephone network & CCTV surveillance system.
  • Supply & Installation of the backup power system (Generators/Solar Inverters).

Routine Electrical Work & Maintenance


We undertake maintenance of
  • Panels and power distribution systems, switchgear, panels, transformers, enclosed switches, etc.
  • All electrical installation work of a routine nature in connection with the maintenance or operation of the building or plant therein performed at frequent intervals.
  • Regular health test of all Electrical equipment and wiring at a regular interval of 4-6 months.

Electrical Repair, Replace & Relocate


We undertake
  • Replacement of electrical devices, spares and equipment.
  • Repair existing equipment, electrical wiring system of building or plant.
  • Relocations of existing equipment from one area of the building to another using the original electrical devices i.e. moving an assembly area or machine tool.

Customized Electrical Works


We undertake maintenance of
  • Electrical works inside plant area, potentially explosive atmosphere, mines, etc.
  • Electrical works within an exhibition, shows, public lighting and stands.
