Genii’s Renewable Energy Initiatives – Serving 15,000 Households across 25 Districts in Odisha, Assam and UP

Genii is associated with Global leaders in the field of Solar Energy to provide best quality systems to our customers, least downtime and ensure quality service. We are actively participating in the Jawaharlal Nehru National Solar Mission under Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE), GOI, New Delhi for promoting Green Energy by executing prestigious projects in both Government and Private sector. The initiative by Govt of India for capacity creation of 100GW Solar Power by 2020 has provided a new thrust to the Solar Program.

Genii’s Commision

GENII has commissioned MWs of On-Grid, Off-Grid, Micro & Mini-Grid Solar PV Power Plants in various states and of various capacities across the country. We are proud to be associated in lighting thousands of remote houses in many villages through Remote Villages Electrification Scheme of Government of India under DDUGJY scheme. Major work includes the construction of control room, setting up of Solar Power Plant, Providing Home Lighting Systems, Laying LT Line from Solar Plant, Renewable Energy Power distribution in the Villages, electrical wiring in each house. Remote Village Electrification is done under Deendayal Upadhyaya Gram Jyoti Yojana (DDUGJY) by REC, Government of India (GOI).

Genii’s Priority

GENII’s current priority is to provide of basic needs such as electricity, drinking water, sanitation & basic health support to these underprivileged people living in the remote locations. Our strength is our vast experience and our existence in every corner of the state. We are proud to be part of the development activities of these remote and rural areas and will remain committed and engaged in future with the latest technology.

We are delighted to be a part of the development process of the people living in remotest areas of our country such as people living in tribal and remote areas in these states. We certainly believe that electrifying these households is just a beginning and this is going to bring about a lot of changes in their lives which includes right to education, employment, sanitation facilities, and healthcare facilities. It’s worthwhile to note that GENII took the initiative as a part of our social responsibility to help the residents of the aforementioned remote areas to get their Aadhaar allotted to them which in the current conditions is a basic requirement to be a beneficiary of any government schemes.
