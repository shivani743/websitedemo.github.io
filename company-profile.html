About Company

Genii engineering & Services (P) Ltd., is played a major role by undertaking Renewable Energy Projects in urban, rural & remote areas of the country. Our Participation in projects initiated by Ministry of New & Renewable Energy (MNRE), Rural Electrification Corporation (REC) is evidence of our dedication towards achieving sustainable energy target.
GENII with its dedicated efforts is intended to create a new and improved technology-driven economy of India, that can provide quality and healthy life to its citizens. The objective of our research and development (R&D) is to acquire knowledge to develop new product verticals, that eventually will result in new or improved products, processes, systems or services that can increase the organization's reach to the public and meet their basic requirements, especially for people living in remote and rural areas.

Genii is one of India's foremost private sector companies with a Gross Sales Value of INR 70.2 Cr. Genii has a diversified presence in Renewable Energy, Infrastructural Works, Electrical Works, Sewage Treatment and IT Services. Genii as major promoter of Renewable Energy in Urban, Rural & Remote area applications of Ministry of New & Renewable Energy (MNRE) & Rural Electrification Corporation (REC) under Ministry Power, Govt of India, and associated with various state and central government project across the country including military and paramilitary forces.

Vision Mission Genii Values

Our vision is to reduce the country's dependence on fossil fuels, reduce carbon emissions, lower energy costs and contribute to creation of substantial capacity in Renewable energy and provide better quality of life to the people.

Genii Engineering & Services (P) Ltd. is committed to provide proven solar power generating systems at most competitive prices incorporating state of the art technology, maintaining high levels of quality and thereby contribute towards increasing the installed capacity of solar power plants in the country.

Genii believes that its core values of trusteeship, customer focus, respect for people, excellence, innovation and nation orientation have been the guiding principles behind the Company's phenomenal growth, helping it to become one of India's most admired and valued enterprises.

FEW MILESTONES


  • Genii has commissioned 1000+ ON-Grid and OFF-Grid Solar PV Power Plants of capacity ranging from 1KW to 1MW aggregating to 15MW across the country.
  • Genii is proud to be associated in lighting up 9992+ numbers of remote houses through Mini & Micro Grid & Standalone Home Lighting Systems in remote villages under DDUGJY & SAUBHAGYA scheme of Rural Electrification Corporation (REC), a flagship program of Govt. of India.
  • Genii is in the process of executing 10000+ All in One LED Street Lighting Systems by the end of March’2021.
  • The company follows International standards for quality management, Environmental Management System, Occupational Health & Safety Management System.
  • As a registered MSME & Startup company has generated 15000+ direct and indirect employment in the country.
  • Professionally managed company to undertake technically diversified projects in power conservation across the country.
  • The company has highly professional management team with expertise in the fields of Design, Engineering, Manufacturing, Execution, Operations and Support Services.
  • Service and support centers located across the country.
